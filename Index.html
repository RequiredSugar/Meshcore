The Network

MeshCore is a decentralized LoRa-based communication platform. Unlike cellular networks, MeshCore operates independently of infrastructure, making it resilient during Roanoke's winter storms or regional power outages.

Default USA Settings

Region: United States (US)
Frequency: 915 MHz ISM Band
Primary Channel: LongFast (Default)
Max Hops: 64 (MeshCore standard)
Center Freq: 906.875 MHz

Ensure your hardware is tuned for 915 MHz. Using antennas tuned for 868 MHz (EU) will result in poor performance and potential hardware damage.

Infrastructure Repeater

Designed for high-elevation placement (e.g., Cave Spring ridges) to act as a backbone. Recommended configuration:

  • Power: 1s2p 18650 battery configuration for extended runtime.
  • Antenna: SMA bulkhead connector to a 5.8dBi tuned fiberglass antenna.
