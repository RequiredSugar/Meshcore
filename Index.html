The Network

MeshCore is a decentralized LoRa-based communication platform. Unlike cellular networks, MeshCore operates independently of infrastructure, making it resilient during Roanoke's winter storms or regional power outages.

Default USA/Canada Settings

Frequency: 910.525 MHz
Bandwidth: 62.5 kHz
Spreading Factor: 7
Coding Rate: 5
Transmit Power: 22 dBm (max effective)

Ensure your antenna is tuned for 915 MHz. Using antennas tuned for 868 MHz (EU) will result in poor performance and potential hardware damage.
